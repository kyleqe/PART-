Our Locations

KH Training Co. operates from two Cape Town locations to make training as accessible as possible.

Green Point Studio

Address: 14 Somerset Road, Green Point, Cape Town, 8005

Type: Indoor personal training studio

Services: 1-on-1 Personal Training, Nutrition Consultations

Hours:

  • Monday - Friday: 06:00 - 20:00
  • Saturday: 07:00 - 14:00
  • Sunday: Closed

Parking: Street parking available on Somerset Road. Paid parking at nearby V&A Waterfront.

Camps Bay - Outdoor Sessions

Address: Camps Bay Beach Promenade, Camps Bay, Cape Town, 8005

Type: Outdoor bootcamp training area

Services: Group Bootcamps (4-8 people)

Session Times:

  • Tuesday & Thursday: 06:30 - 07:30
  • Saturday: 08:00 - 09:00

Note: Sessions are weather dependent. Kyle will notify participants of any cancellations via WhatsApp at least 1 hour in advance.
