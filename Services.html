Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any fitness experience to start?

Not at all. Kyle works with complete beginners and experienced athletes alike. Every programme is built around your current fitness level and progressed at a pace that's right for you.

Where are the training sessions held?

Personal training sessions are held at the KH Training Co. studio in Green Point, Cape Town. Group bootcamps are held outdoors at the Camps Bay Beach Promenade. Online coaching is fully remote.

What should I bring to my first session?

Just comfortable training clothes, a water bottle, and a positive attitude. All equipment is provided at the studio. For outdoor bootcamps, we recommend sunscreen and a small towel.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients notice improved energy levels and movement quality within 2-3 weeks. Visible physical changes typically begin around 6-8 weeks with consistent training and nutrition. Results vary based on individual commitment and goals.

Can I cancel or reschedule sessions?

Yes - sessions can be rescheduled with 24 hours notice at no charge. Late cancellations (under 24 hours) may be subject to a 50% session fee.
