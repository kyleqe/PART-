About Kyle Hove

Coach, founder, and the driving force behind KH Training Co.

My Story

From Passion to Profession

Kyle Hove grew up in Cape Town with a deep love for sport and movement. After years of competitive rugby and personal fitness, he realised that the most powerful tool he had wasn't just physical strength it was the ability to help others discover theirs.

In 2019, Kyle completed his personal training certification through REPSSA (Register of Exercise Professionals South Africa) and spent two years coaching at commercial gyms across the city. In 2021, he took the leap and founded KH Training Co.,opening a small but dedicated studio in Green Point.

Today, Kyle has coached over 200 clients from desk-bound professionals looking to move better, to athletes seeking peak performance. His philosophy is simple: train with purpose, recover with intention, and never stop improving.

Coaching Philosophy

Every programme Kyle builds is guided by three core principles.

Personalisation First

No two bodies are the same. Every client receives a programme built around their unique goals, movement patterns, lifestyle, and schedule - not a generic template.

Progressive Overload

Sustainable results come from consistent, measurable progression. Kyle tracks every session to ensure clients are always moving forward never stuck on a plateau.

Mindset & Lifestyle

Fitness is more than what happens in the gym. Kyle coaches clients on recovery, nutrition habits, and the mental resilience needed to make fitness a lifelong commitment.
