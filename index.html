Meet Your Coach

Kyle Hove - Head Coach & Founder

Kyle Hove founded KH Training Co. in 2021 with a simple belief: sustainable fitness comes from understanding your body, not punishing it. Based in Cape Town, Kyle has coached over 200 clients from complete beginners to competitive athletes.

His approach combines evidence based strength training, movement quality, and mindset coaching to help clients achieve lasting physical transformation.
